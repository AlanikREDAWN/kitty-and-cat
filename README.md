# Kitty and Cat

Kitty and Cat are both selfbots that live in Hack Club's Slack! They mainly hang out in #kitty-cat-playground, so you can visit and play with them there!

## Things they can do:

### Respond to greetings

If you send a greeting (something like hey, hello, or hi) with their name (Kitty or Cat), then they'll respond and say hello back!

### Meow with you

If you meow or make a cat noise, they'll make a cat noise back! yay, best meowing buddies!

### Change their statuses

If you send a message with their name (Kitty or Cat), the word "status", and the status you want them to change to, they'll change it for you!

#### Available status keywords

- none

- doppel

- party

- wow

- boom

### Chat with you

If you say their name (Kitty or Cat), and you don't include a greeting, a cat noise, or the word "status", they'll chat with you and respond based on what you say! (powered by [Hack Club AI](https://ai.hackclub.com))
